        <h3 id="timeline">His life and career at a glance...</h3>
        <ul id="timeline">
          <li><strong>1930</strong> Born in New York, NY
          </li>
          <li><strong>1940</strong> Began his friendship with Oscar Hammerstein II
          </li>
          <li><strong>1950</strong> Graduate magna cum laude from Williams College 
          </li>
          <li><strong>1954</strong> Met Leonard Bernstein and began working as a lyricist for <em>West Side Story</em>
          </li>
          <li><strong>1959</strong> Collaborated with Jule Styne and wrote lyrics for <em>Gypsy</em>
          </li>
          <li><strong>1962</strong> <em>A Funny Thing Happened on the Way to the Forum</em> opened on Broadway, the first musical for which Sondheim wrote both music and lyrics
          </li>
          <li><strong>1970</strong> Began his collaboration with Hal Prince
          </li>
          <li><strong>1973</strong> Elected as the sixteenth president of the Dramatist's Guild of America
          </li>
          <li><strong>1984</strong> Began his collaboration with James Lapine
          </li>
          <li><strong>2017</strong> Married Jeffrey Scott Romley
          </li>
          <li><strong>2021</strong> Died at his home in Roxbury, Connecticut
          </li>
        </ul>
